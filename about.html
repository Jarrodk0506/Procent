Pro%Cent is two commercial flooring software programs that can be linked together!


One is designed for your Sales Team and the other one is for your Accounting & Financial people. Both can be seamlessly linked for maximum efficiency or you can use the front end sales process as a standalone program since this was created originally as sales & project management relational database for our sales people so we could replace all those assorted Excel spread sheets the sales people were forced to use. Our administrators now see every bid and all activity.


The Sales and Project Management front end is the core of the program and is designed to keep your sales people out of trouble before you sell the job then it will give them the finest commercial flooring project management and communication process ever developed when they start running a the job. Pro%Cent allows them to handle more business and includes many accounting features; it’s like each sales person gets a sales assistant. Do not confuse this with an electronic quantity estimating program.
